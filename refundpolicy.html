Refund / Exchange Policy

Our goal is to provide quality fitting products for all of our customers. We will provide exchanges for ill fitting products ordered in your Dancebox.

If you need to return your purchase for a refund or exchange, please email info@shopdancebox.com for the shipping address and return information. We will respond within two business days. Returns are due within 30 days of purchase and must be in new, unwashed, and unworn condition with tags (if applicable) attached.

All purchases are final after 30 days.

Please try on leotards with underwear to be eligible for return, refund or exchange

Returns accepted on unwashed, unworn leotards, shorts, skirts, and shoes within 30 days

Shipping fees are non-refundable
